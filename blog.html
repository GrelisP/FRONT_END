Reg tech By Titas Aleksandravicius - financial reporting enthusiast on March 21, 2017. What is regulatory technology or better knows as Regtech and why it has great potential? To better understand it, we need to look at the reasons and context which lead to the growth of Regtech. Firstly after the 2008 financial crisis, people and politicialns were not happy with the performance of the financial sector. This lead to an increase of regulation. Secondly, there is a growth of fintech companies which put presure on the brick and mortart companies of the financial sector. One of the competiteve advantages of the fintech is the create financial products with the newest techologies and offer better deals.
