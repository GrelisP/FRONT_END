Silerg was one of the first investors in Lyft in its early rounds, he is also the co-founder and former CEO of both Snapfish (acquired by HP in 2005) and Fitmob (acquired by ClassPass in 2015), as well as the managing director of Mayfield Fund. Lyft is the main competitor of UBER in the US and Canada.
