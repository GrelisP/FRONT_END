Contests and Events

8th Defcon Bike Ride Event Friday: 0600 At 6am on Friday, the @cycle_override crew will be hosting the 8th Defcon Bikeride. We'll meet at a local bikeshop, get some rental bicycles, and about 7am will make the ride out to Red Rocks. It's about a 15 mile ride, all downhill on the return journey. So, if you are crazy enough to join us, get some water, and head over to cycleoverride.org for more info. See at 6am Friday! @jp_bourget @gdead @heidishmoo. Go to cycleoverride.org for more info. More Info: on Twitter, cycleoverride

AI Village Jeopardy! Contest | Location: AI Village Friday: AI Village Hours In a jeopardy style CTF, contestants (if we are provided the full space requested) will be able to compete and learn by working through our challenges categorized in groups such as “classification”, “clustering”, and “attacking machine learning models’. Participants will be provided a docker image will all datasets, questions (in IPython notebooks), and submission APIs needed to compete. Educational materials will be provided for initial (novice level) challenges to ensure that all contestants have a baseline understanding of the core concepts needed to compete. More Info: @aivillage_dc, jeopardy-ctf.aivillage.org

BadgeLife Contest Contest | Location: Contest Floor All Con Badges have been around Defcon for years, and badge hacking happens every year! This year, let’s make it an official contest! Let’s award prizes! Let’s judge badges on originality, functionality, best counterfeit, and our personal favorite OMGWTFBBQ! More Info: badgelife.org, @dcbadgelife

Capture the Packet Contest | Location: Car Hacking Village Regular CHV Hours CHV CTF is a good ole fashion jeopardy style CTF that challenges your Car Hacking knowledge and prowess. This year we will also incorporate building autonomous cars, Trunk Escape, and Drink don't Drive. Plus other hardware, software and RF related challenges. Oh and in case you thought we weren't above bribes, the winner will get a big prize. Rules and info will be posted before Def Con at CarHackingVillage.com/ctf More Info: carhackingvillage.com/ctf
